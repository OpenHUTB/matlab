Driving Scenario Designer的键盘快捷方式和鼠标操作

注意

在 Macintosh 平台上，使用 Command (⌘) 键而不是 Ctrl 。

画布操作

These operations apply when you edit scenarios or sensors on the Scenario Canvas or Sensor Canvas panes, respectively.

Task Action
Cut road, actor, barrier, or sensor Ctrl+X
Copy road, actor, barrier, or sensor Ctrl+C
Paste road, actor, barrier, or sensor Ctrl+V
Delete road, actor, barrier, or sensor Delete
Undo Ctrl+Z
Redo Ctrl+Y
Zoom in or out Scroll wheel

Road Operations

These operations apply when you add or edit roads on the Scenario Canvas pane.

Task Action
Move road one meter in any direction Up, down, left, and right arrows
Commit a road to the canvas at the last-clicked road center Press Enter or right-click in the canvas while creating the road
Commit a road to the canvas and create a road center at the current location

Double-click in the canvas while creating the road

A new road center is committed at the point where you double-click.
Exit the road editing mode and remove any road centers added while editing Esc
Add a road center to an existing road Double-click the selected road at the point where you want to add the road center

Actor Operations

Actor Selection, Placement, and Movement

These operations apply after you add actors to the Scenario Canvas pane.

Task Action
Select an actor Click
Select multiple actors

Ctrl+click

或者，按住 Shift 并在演员周围画一个框想要选择。要选择角色，参与者来源必须是 在盒子里。
Align actors along a selected dimension

右键单击所选的演员之一，然后在 Align Actors 菜单中选择一个选项。您可以沿着演员的顶部、底部、左侧或右侧对齐演员，或者沿着它们的水平或垂直中心对齐。这个图示显示了沿着演员的左侧对齐的情况。
Distribute selected actors evenly along a road

右键单击所选的演员之一，然后在 Distribute Actors 菜单中选择一个选项。您可以水平或垂直分布演员。这个图示显示了演员沿着一条道路垂直分布的情况。
Move selected actors 1 meter in any direction Up, down, left, and right arrows
Move selected actors 0.1 meter in any direction Hold Ctrl and use the up, down, left, and right arrows

Actor Trajectories

These operations apply after you select an actor on the Scenario Canvas pane and right-click the actor to add trajectory waypoints. If you select multiple actors, then these operations are disabled.

Task Action
将轨迹提交到画布上的最后点击的路径点 在创建轨迹时，按下 Enter 键或右键。
将演员的轨迹提交到画布，并在当前位置创建一个路径点

在创建轨迹时，在画布上双击

在您双击的位置提交一个新的路径点
退出轨迹编辑模式，并删除在编辑过程中添加的任何路径点 Esc
向现有轨迹添加一个路径点 在您想要添加路径点的位置，双击选定的演员

向轨迹添加前进运动的路径点

 Press Ctrl+F and add new waypoints
向轨迹添加反向运动的路径点 Press Ctrl+R and add new waypoints to trajectory

Actor Rotation

这些操作适用于在Scenario Canvas 窗格上的演员，这些演员尚未具有指定的轨迹。要修改所选演员的现有轨迹，请交互式地移动演员的路径点。或者，在 Actors 窗格上，编辑 Waypoints, Speeds, Wait Times, and Yaw 的表格。

要交互式旋转一个尚未具有轨迹的演员，请将指针移动到演员上，并选择演员旋转小部件。

如果您没有看到此小部件，请尝试放大。

或者，点击窗格以选择要旋转的演员，并使用以下键盘快捷键。

如果您选择了多个演员，则这些操作将应用于所有选定的演员。

Task Action
Rotate actor 1 degree clockwise Hold Alt and press the right arrow key
Rotate actor 1 degree counterclockwise Hold Alt and press the left arrow key
Rotate actor 15 degrees clockwise Hold Alt+Ctrl and press the right arrow key
Rotate actor 15 degrees counterclockwise Hold Alt+Ctrl and press the left arrow key
Set actor rotation to 0 degrees Hold Alt and press the up arrow key
Set actor rotation to 180 degrees Hold Alt and press the down arrow key

Preview Actor Times of Arrival

当驾驶场景中至少有一个演员具有指定的等待时间或非默认的生成和消失时间时，此操作将应用。要为演员指定等待时间或生成和消失时间，请分别在 Waypoints, Speeds, Wait Times, and Yaw table 中使用 wait (s) 参数，或使用Actor spawn and despawn 参数。

Task Action
Preview actor times of arrival at waypoints

Point to a waypoint along the trajectory of an actor

Barrier Placement Operations

These operations apply while you add barriers to the Scenario Canvas pane using Add Actor > Jersey Barrier or Add Actor > Guardrail.

Task Action
向单个道路边缘添加障碍。 Single Click on the highlighted road edge.
向多个道路边缘添加障碍物。

Ctrl+Click on the highlighted road edge.

Continue to add barriers to multiple road edges as required.
选择道路同一侧的多个路段。

Shift+Click on a road edge.

If any intersections exist, barriers will not be added to the area of intersection.
